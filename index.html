Bay Area Armenian Genocide Centennial Committee

Events

Cultural

  • Photo Exhibit by an award-winning, renowned photographer.
  • Concert by an internationally recognized singer. This concert will coincide with an artistic display of 10,000 shoes entitled "Shoes for Humanity and Justice".
  • San Francisco performance on April 24th by the Arax Dance Ensemble led by choreographer Noemi Arax Keledjian.
  • A film festival focused on the Armenian Genocide.

Educational

  • A major international conference, combined with a lecture series, on the Armenian Genocide.
  • The Genocide Education Project will organize three workshops on the meaning of the Centennial for public school teachers.

Religious

  • Ecumenical Divine Liturgy at the Mount Davidson Cross, bringing together clergy from all churches in a special Requiem Service.

Awareness

  • A cocktail/reception for the San Francisco Bay Area region’s political and community leaders.
  • Throughout April, massive advertisement, including online.
